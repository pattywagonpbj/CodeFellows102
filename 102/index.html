Condition ;IGY6 Mission

Condition ;IGY6 is an organization where half of its profits help veterans/disabled veterans or first responders that need help. At Condition ;IGY6, we focus on the mission and believe of the importance behind it. We dedicate our time, money and effort to helping organizations like the Wounded Warrior Foundation or veterans that are in serious situations and that need help. We also help out veterans that are struggling with depression, addiction, and other cases like suicide.

Condition ;IGY6 takes pride in the veterans and first responders that we help on a day to day basis. We have already helped 312,222 disabled veterans in the Wounded Warrior Foundation by giving them jobs in the company or helping them out with donations to get them off of the streets and into a home that they can call their own. With your purchase in one of our select stores or donations, just remember that you are helping out the men and women who served your country.

Thank you for your support. We at Condition ;IGY6 and all of the veterans and first responders and more than thankful.
